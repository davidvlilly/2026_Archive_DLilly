Retirement Simulation - Calculated Column Definitions
=====================================================

ira_pull
--------
Amount withdrawn from IRA during the year.
- Minimum: Required Minimum Distribution (RMD) if age >= 72
- Additional: If standard account can't cover (draw + taxes - SS), pulls more from IRA
- Capped at available IRA balance
Formula: max(RMD, amount_needed_to_cover_shortfall), limited to ira_beg

roth_conv
---------
Amount converted from IRA to Roth during the year.
- Only occurs if roth_conv_threshold > 0 and ord_inc < threshold
- Converts enough to bring ordinary income up to the threshold
- Capped at (ira_beg - ira_pull)
Formula: min(roth_conv_threshold - base_ord_inc, available_ira_after_pull)

roth_pull
---------
Emergency withdrawal from Roth if standard account goes negative.
- Only triggered when std_end would be < 0 after all other calculations
- Tax-free withdrawal
Formula: min(shortfall, roth_end)

total_inc
---------
Total income for the year (AGI equivalent).
Formula: ord_inc + pref_inc

ord_inc
-------
Ordinary income - taxed at federal bracket rates (10-37%).
Components:
- SS_inc (Social Security)
- interest_income (fixed_ROR on std fixed portion)
- ira_pull
- roth_conv (conversions are taxable as ordinary income)
Formula: SS_inc + interest_income + ira_pull + roth_conv
Note: Dividends are NOT included here (they go to pref_inc)

pref_inc
--------
Preferential income - taxed at 0/15/20% rates.
Components:
- div_income (1.5% dividend yield on std stock portion - treated as qualified)
- LTCapGn (realized long-term capital gains from std withdrawals)
Formula: div_income + LTCapGn

ord_tax
-------
Federal tax on ordinary income using bracket rates.
Formula: calc_federal_tax_single(ord_inc - standard_deduction)
Where standard_deduction = $14,600

pref_tax
--------
Tax on preferential income at 0/15/20% rates.
- Stacked on top of ordinary taxable income to determine rate
- 0% up to $47,025 total taxable
- 15% from $47,026 to $518,900
- 20% above $518,900
Formula: calc_pref_tax(pref_inc, ord_taxable)

total_tax
---------
Total federal tax for the year.
Formula: ord_tax + pref_tax

total_sav
---------
Sum of all account balances at year end.
Formula: ira_end + std_end + roth_end

amount_avail
------------
After-tax value if everything were liquidated at year end.
- IRA: fully taxable as ordinary income
- Std: only stock gains taxable (as preferential income)
- Roth: tax-free
Formula: total_sav - (tax_on_ira_liquidation + pref_tax_on_std_gains)

std_stock_gain
--------------
Growth of the stock portion of standard account during the year.
Formula: (std_beg * stock_pct) * stock_ROR

std_stock_basis
---------------
Cost basis of stocks in standard account at year end.
- Tracks original purchase cost for capital gains calculation
- Reduces proportionally when stocks are sold
Formula: previous_basis - (basis_portion_of_withdrawal)

std_taxable_gain
----------------
Unrealized taxable gain in standard account stocks at year end.
Formula: (std_end * stock_pct) - std_stock_basis

LTCapGn
-------
Long-term capital gains realized during the year from std withdrawals.
- Only applies to stock portion of withdrawals
- Taxable portion based on (gain / total_value) ratio
Formula: (std_withdrawal * stock_pct) * (std_taxable_gain_beg / stock_value_beg)

total_gains
-----------
All investment gains and income for the year.
Formula: ira_growth + std_growth + roth_growth + div_income + interest_income

total_expenses
--------------
Spending plus taxes for the year.
Formula: draw + total_tax

net_gain_loss
-------------
Net change from investments minus expenses.
Formula: total_gains - total_expenses

marg_rate
---------
Marginal tax rate on the next dollar of ordinary income.
Based on 2024 brackets: 10%, 12%, 22%, 24%, 32%, 35%, 37%
Determined by where ord_taxable falls in bracket structure.
Rounded to nearest 1%.

tax_rate
--------
Effective (average) tax rate for the year.
Formula: total_tax / total_inc
